Chicken Nuggets

Description

These delicious nuggets are breaded in an egg and dijon mustard wash before being coated with herb seasoned bread crumbs and then fried in olive oil. My kids love them!

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 TBSP Dijon Mustard
  • 1/2lb. Chicken Breast
  • 1 1/2 cup Bread Crumbs
  • 1/4 tsp. Poultry Seasoining
  • 1/2 tsp. Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 tsp. Onion Powder
  • 3 TBSP Parmesan Cheese
  • Pinch Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil over medium heat.
  2. Pound chicken slightly and cut into 1 inch cubes.
  3. Mix eggs and dijon mustard in bowl.
  4. Mix remaining ingredients except olive oil together on a shallow plate.
  5. Dip chicken in egg mixture and then in bread crumb mixture. Set aside until all pieces are breaded.
  6. Fry chicken in olive oil about 3-5 minutes per side or until golden brown.
  7. Enjoy!
