Carbonara

Description

Created by Gina Homolka, this skinny version of the traditionally heavy sauced carbonara is lightened up with chicken broth and a sprinkling of cheese.

Poached eggs and a sprinkle of bacon on top finish this delicious dish!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Chicken broth
  • Arugula
  • Grilled Chicken
  • Spaghetti
  • Parmeggiano Regiano Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
