Carbonara

Description

This skinny version of the traditionally heavy sauced carbonara is lightened up with chicken broth and a sprinkling of cheese.

Poached eggs and a sprinkle of bacon on top finish this delicious dish! Feel free to subsitute shrimp for chicken.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Slices Bacon
  • 2/3 Cup Chicken broth
  • 55 grams Arugula
  • 1/2lb Grilled Chicken
  • 4 oz Spaghetti
  • 27 grams Parmeggiano Regiano Cheese
  • Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Put a pot of water on the stove for the spaghetti and also a small pot on the stove for poaching the eggs.
  2. In a large skillet, cook bacon until fully cooked leaving the grease behind.
  3. When water comes to a boil, put in salt and pasta and cook 7-8 minutes; drain.
  4. Meanwhile, pour chicken broth in with bacon grease over medium heat and simmer until reduced by half.
  5. When water for eggs comes to a boil, put in 6 tablespoons of vinegar and 1/4 tsp of kosher salt. Crack egg into small ramiken dish and gently pour it into the boiling solution. Simmer for 2 minutes then remove onto plate. Repeat with second egg.
  6. Add arugula, salt and pepper to skillet with chicken broth and bacon grease. Cook for 3 minutes
  7. Add spaghetti and cheese to skillet and cook until cheese is melted.
  8. Pour contents of skillet onto a plate and then put poached eggs, grilled chicken and bacon on top.
  9. Enjoy!
